Hi, I’m Eshwar. I’m a PhD student in the Department of Computer and Information Science at the University of Pennsylvania. I'm fortunate to be advised by Sampath Kannan and Anindya De.

I work in theoretical computer science and am interested in algorithms and complexity, particularly algorithmic game theory, learning theory, online algorithms, and algorithmic fairness. Currently, I’m visiting the Simons Institute for the Theory of Computing at UC Berkeley, participating in the Special Year on Large Language Models and Transformers: Part 1.

My research was previously supported by a gift from AWS AI for research in Trustworthy AI.
