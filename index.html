Highlights:

  • June 2025: Started as a Quantitative Associate at SESCO Enterprises, researching electricity markets.
  • June 2025: Completed my PhD in the Department of Computer and Information Science at the University of Pennsylvania, advised by Sampath Kannan and Anindya De.
  • Research covered in Quanta Magazine, reprinted in WIRED, and discussed on the Quanta Podcast.
  • Best Paper and Best Student Paper, EC 2025, for “Swap Regret and Correlated Equilibria Beyond Normal-Form Games.”

I work in theoretical computer science and am interested in algorithms and complexity, particularly algorithmic game theory, learning theory, online algorithms, and algorithmic fairness. I recently finished a visit to the Simons Institute for the Theory of Computing at UC Berkeley as part of the Special Year on Large Language Models and Transformers: Part 1.

My research was previously supported by a gift from AWS AI for research in Trustworthy AI.
